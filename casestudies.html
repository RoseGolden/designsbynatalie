Case Studies

Good Luxe

Good Luxe is an e-commerce website that features women’s clothing that are both high in quality and bold in appearance. Our brand focuses on timeless styles that are focused to fit your personal preference. Our designs are unique and fun, yet are mature and accessible for the modern woman.

Fanfare

Fanfare is a mobile app that provides a fast and easy way for young adults to find tickets to their favorite artist’s concernts, as well as find other artists that match their interests. In today’s diverse music industry, we make the process of purchasing tickets as simple as possible so you can focus more on enjoying the show.

Blocbox

Blocbox is a web app that allows you collect and sort information. Create simple notes, store images and add links. Users will no longer have to sift through bookmarks and saved images through countless folders. With Blocbox, all of your information remainsin one convinient place.
